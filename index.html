My Intro to Coding

I never planned on becoming a programmer. My teacher in high school just noticed I had a knack for it and encouraged me to take more classes. Somewhere between solving little puzzles and watching my ideas come alive on screen, I fell in love with the process. What I enjoy most now is the freedom to make anything I can imagine, and that moment when all the pieces finally fit together. The end result is truly rewarding, and it’s like the story I’ve been writing in code suddenly breathes.
